# Vertex Group plc — Financial Due Diligence Summary FY2024
**STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL — Project EMBER**
Prepared by: EY Transaction Advisory Services | June 2024

## Executive Summary

Vertex Group plc ("Vertex" or "the Company") is a UK-headquartered specialty fashion retailer operating 312 stores across the UK, Ireland, and the Netherlands. The company was taken private in a GBP 1.4B leveraged buyout by Meridian Capital Partners in October 2019 at an entry multiple of 7.8x EV/EBITDA.

Following severe disruption from the COVID-19 pandemic, sustained post-pandemic shifts in consumer spending toward value and online-first brands, and a failed omnichannel transformation programme (Project Nova), the Company is in a state of acute financial distress.

FY2024 revenues declined 18.3% year-on-year to GBP 612M. EBITDA was negative at -GBP 19.6M (-3.2% margin), the second consecutive year of negative EBITDA. The Company is in breach of its senior secured leverage covenant and has engaged Alvarez & Marsal as Chief Restructuring Officer. Available liquidity headroom stands at approximately GBP 12M — representing approximately 6 weeks of operating runway at the current burn rate.

This report identifies material going concern risk. Investment Committee approval at the enhanced governance threshold is required before any binding commitment.
